What is a Growth Mindset?

A growth mindset is the belief that your talents and abilities can be developed through dedication, hard work, and feedback. It encourages learning and resilience, leading to greater success.

Psychologist Carol Dweck introduced the concept, contrasting it with a fixed mindset‚Äîwhere people believe abilities are static. A growth mindset creates a passion for learning rather than a hunger for approval.

How to Develop It?

Start by embracing challenges, persist through obstacles, and replace the word "failing" with the phrase "learning opportunity." Stay curious and always ask, "What can I learn from this?"

Use positive self-talk, seek feedback regularly, and surround yourself with people who support your growth. Keep a journal of your learning moments, track progress, and reward effort, not just outcomes.
